Stream Specific Commands
=============

Different commands that come into play when playing specific types games

Nancy Drew (and other streams with iPad notes)
----------------------------------------------

**!shownotes**
  Take a closer look at the notes that are on my iPad

**!gameplease**
  Send the notes view back to its original place.
  
  Star Citizen
  ------------
  
  **!enlist**
    Get my Star Citizen referral link
  
  **!fleet**
    Get a link to my fleet

Duo games with Tom (aka Phat32)
-------------------------------
*Limited to Subs and Mods*

**!wthtom**
  Pull up Tom's game feed to see what's happening on his screen

**!undo**
  Put the stream back to normal

**!gtfo**
  Hide Tom's face from the duo stream.
